About Us

Welcome To Capulus Cafe

Little Story

Founded in 2019 on the busy street of Sindhu Bhavan Road, Ahmedabad, by a group of 3 college friends with a belief and a mission to incorporate the coffee-culture in the community and to stir a social revolution, ‘Capulus’ gets its name from the reviews of the food it serves - simply, excellent.

Capulus is Ahmedabad’s first completely indigenous and eclectic café, known not just for its menu, but its eccentric social setting and a highly happening ambience. Celebrating 2 years of operations, it has hosted a fair amount of social events, have awarded Times Food Award twice and influenced the world-view of an entire generation.
